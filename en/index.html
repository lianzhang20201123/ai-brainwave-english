FAQ

FAQ

Key questions about outcomes, safety, scheduling, and retraining.

Is the system safe? Does it emit radiation?

It uses specific acoustic frequency guidance rather than electromagnetic radiation, and the system passed third-party EMC testing.

What if students are already busy with schoolwork?

Training can be arranged as 2-hour sessions on weekdays, concentrated sessions on weekends, and intensive blocks during vacations.

What if words are forgotten after the program?

Each program follows a 6-month service cycle and includes up to 3 years of free retraining.

Who can use it beyond primary and secondary school students?

It also supports college students, adult learners, CET-4/6 preparation, and IELTS/TOEFL vocabulary development.
