Study rhythm

K12 pathway

School term weekends 4–8 hours with split sessions and structured new-word input.
Weekday support 2-hour sessions once or twice a week after school.
Holiday intensives 5–7 continuous days for step-change growth.
Study rhythm

University / adult pathway

Weekends 6–8 concentrated hours when schedules allow.
Weekdays Shorter reinforcement blocks when possible.
Suitable goals CET-4, CET-6, postgraduate exams, overseas study, and workplace English.
Support window

One payment, up to three years of retraining support

The standard service cycle is six months and includes hardware access, the training system, coaching supervision, and community support. If vocabulary control is not yet stable, retraining can be booked within three years.

Assessment first makes program choice easier

A baseline assessment helps clarify phonics, current vocabulary status, and a sensible starting chapter before a learner commits to a full plan.
