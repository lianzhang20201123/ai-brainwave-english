Partnership invitation

We provide programs, equipment, and coach training; you provide the local scene, enrolment, and team

After model verification across several cities, AI Brainwave English is open to education organizations with real brand influence, stable enrolment, and operational capacity.

Partnership model

Project positioningCreate a local AI Brainwave English vocabulary center together.
Revenue sharingTuition revenue is settled through a jointly managed structure and distributed by agreement.
Best fitK12, exam prep, postgraduate, language, or overseas-study organizations with local execution strength.

Programs, equipment, training, and operations support

EquipmentComplete training hardware with installation, maintenance, and iteration support.
ProgramsStandardized program system from K12 to TOEFL and IELTS.
Coach trainingHeadquarters support for training local coaches and supervisors.
OperationsUnified brand assets, lead-generation materials, enrollment scripts, and learner management support.
What the local partner provides

Local implementation conditions

Dedicated teaching spaceRoughly 100 square meters, enough for 6–8 learning units.
Enrollment and local demandExisting student resources and operational channels for local conversion.
On-site teamAt least one full-time local operator responsible for daily delivery and customer service.
Supporting toolsBasic devices, interior setup, and normal site management capability.

Designed for mature education organizations

Basic conditionOrganizations with stable revenue and a clear local base.
Space and teamIndependent teaching space and recruitment capacity.
AlignmentWillingness to invest in neuroscience-oriented education and local execution.
