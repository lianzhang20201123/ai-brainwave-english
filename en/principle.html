Principles

How the system works

AI Brainwave English combines a dedicated learning environment, acoustic guidance, staged review, and data-backed retraining to support stronger long-term vocabulary retention.

Three layers

Hardware, data, and intelligence

Hardware layer — recliners, headsets, tablets, and controlled learning cabins reduce distraction and improve comfort.
Data layer — the system tracks vocabulary mastery, learning behavior, and forgetting patterns.
Intelligence layer — training content, pacing, and review schedules are adjusted according to progress.

Three-layer structure

A system approach rather than a single memorization tool.

Closed-loop training

Assessment, training, review, and retraining support better long-term retention.

Dedicated learning cabin

The environment is designed to lower distraction and support consistent vocabulary input.

Group training scenes

Suitable for schools, classes, and staged intensive training.

Visible learning path

Testing and review make each stage easier to understand for families and schools.

Assessment first

Start from the current level

Phonics, vocabulary, and learner fit are assessed before a study plan is recommended.

Training

Focused vocabulary input

The system reduces noise, improves comfort, and supports more sustained vocabulary learning.

Retention

Review and retraining

Staged review and up to 3 years of free retraining help preserve what has been learned.
