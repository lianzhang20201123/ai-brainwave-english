Principles

Core principles

Built around learning cabins, acoustic guidance, data analysis, and a closed training loop.

Hardware layer

Dedicated recliners, headphones, tablets, and a controlled environment reduce distraction and improve comfort.

Data layer

Learning behavior, vocabulary mastery, and forgetting curves are recorded to build personal learning profiles.

Intelligence layer

The system dynamically recommends vocabulary sets, review pacing, and training content for each learner.

Training loop

Assessment, training, review, and retraining form a closed loop that supports stronger long-term retention.

Learning cabin environment

Dedicated recliners, headsets, and tablets support a more stable, low-distraction setting for vocabulary training.

Specific acoustic guidance

A non-invasive method that supports a state more suitable for vocabulary input and review.

Data tracking and retraining

Learning records, staged reassessment, and retraining make up the long-term retention maintenance system.
